# CATIA-Project-6-Axis-Robotic-Arm


## 📌 Overview

Designed and assembled a 6-axis robotic arm using CATIA V5 to understand multi-degree-of-freedom motion, joint mechanisms, and industrial automation systems.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Model robotic arm components in CATIA
* Assemble multiple joints with proper constraints
* Understand motion of multi-axis systems

---

## ⚙️ Features

* 6 Degrees of Freedom (DOF)
* Rotational joints
* End-effector movement
* Assembly constraints

---

## 🔧 Components

* Base
* Shoulder joint
* Elbow joint
* Wrist joints
* End effector

---

## 🚀 Applications

* Industrial automation
* Pick and place systems
* Assembly operations
