# 🔄 Working of Robotic Arm

## 🧠 Principle

The robotic arm works based on multiple rotational joints that allow movement in different directions.

---

## ⚙️ Motion Flow

* Base rotates (horizontal movement)
* Shoulder lifts the arm
* Elbow extends/retracts
* Wrist provides orientation
* End effector performs task

---

## 🎯 Output

Combined motion results in precise positioning of the end effector.
